Please find attached message which is required by Authorities to grant ISPS clearance for your vessel's forthcoming call at OPL Durban. 

 

Please take note that your ISPS Application is to be sent at least 96 hours prior to arrival or even earlier at the first South African port including OPL call. Kindly copy operations@portagent.co.za on your email to PAN.

 

The Pre Arrival Notification format has five sheets, i.e.

that needs to be completed.

Sheet 1                    Column A to U - Z

Sheet 2                    Column W – Crew List

Sheet 3                    Column X -  Passenger list

Sheet 4                    Column Y – Other persons

Sheet 5                    Column YS – Security Personnel

 

These sheets must be clicked on and filled in correctly by yourself following the example.  This must be sent as an attachment (Excel Format) to MRCC Cape Town on email pan@samsa.org.za 96 hours prior to ETA.  If PAN is sent to the incorrect email, MRCC will not receive it and this will delay clearance of the vessel.
